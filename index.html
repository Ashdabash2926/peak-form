The Trainer

James Carter

I started my fitness journey at 19 — overweight, low on confidence, and without a clue where to begin. After years of trial, error, and thousands of hours of study, I turned my personal transformation into a career built on one mission: helping others change their lives through the power of smart, consistent training.

Since opening Peak Form in Leeds in 2016, I've worked with everyone from complete beginners to competitive athletes. My approach combines science-backed programming with genuine accountability — because results require both.

Common Questions

Getting started is simple. Fill in the consultation form below and James will get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a free 30-minute call. During that call, you'll discuss your goals, current fitness level, and lifestyle, and James will recommend the most appropriate programme. If you decide to proceed, you'll receive your bespoke plan within 48 hours. There's no obligation and no hard sell — just an honest conversation.
Your first month is about building solid foundations and learning your body. Expect an initial assessment covering movement patterns, baseline fitness, and goal-setting. Sessions in weeks one and two are deliberately moderate — James needs to understand how you recover and respond before pushing intensity. By weeks three and four, you'll be training hard, and most clients report noticeable changes in energy levels and body composition within the first three to four weeks.
